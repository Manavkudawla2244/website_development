Our Facilities

1. Swimming Pool Complex

Enjoy our expansive swimming pool complex, complete with lap lanes, a Jacuzzi, and a dedicated children’s area. Whether you’re looking to exercise or relax, our pool area offers the perfect aquatic experience.

2. Unlimited Culinary Delights

Indulge in a culinary journey with our unlimited food options. From gourmet meals to healthy snacks and refreshing beverages, our chefs cater to all tastes and preferences throughout the day.

3. Spa and Wellness Center

Rejuvenate your body and mind at our luxurious spa. Choose from a variety of massages, facials, and wellness treatments designed to enhance relaxation and promote overall well-being.

4. Fitness Center

Stay active and fit at our state-of-the-art fitness center. Equipped with modern machines and personalized training programs, our fitness facilities cater to both beginners and fitness enthusiasts.

5. Nature Trails and Walking Paths

Immerse yourself in nature with our scenic walking paths and nature trails. Perfect for leisurely strolls or invigorating hikes, these paths offer breathtaking views of our lush green surroundings.

6. Yoga and Meditation Classes

Balance your mind and body with daily yoga and meditation classes. Led by experienced instructors, these sessions are designed to enhance flexibility, reduce stress, and promote inner peace.

7. Tennis and Basketball Courts

Engage in friendly competitions on our tennis and basketball courts. Whether you’re a seasoned player or trying your hand at a new sport, our courts provide the ideal venue for active recreation.

8. Kids' Club and Activities

Entertain your little ones with our Kids' Club, offering supervised activities and games throughout the day. From arts and crafts to outdoor adventures, there’s something for every child to enjoy.

9. Evening Entertainment

Experience lively evening entertainment with live music, themed dinners, and cultural performances. Our events promise unforgettable moments and a vibrant atmosphere for all guests.

10. Business and Conference Facilities

Host successful meetings and conferences in our well-equipped business center. With modern amenities and professional services, we ensure productive gatherings tailored to your needs.

11. Beach Access and Water Sports

Savor the sun and sea with direct beach access. Enjoy a range of water sports activities such as snorkeling, kayaking, and parasailing, perfect for adventure seekers of all ages.

12. Golf Course

Challenge yourself on our scenic golf course, featuring lush fairways and challenging greens. Whether you’re a seasoned golfer or a novice, our course offers an enjoyable round for all skill levels.

13. Cultural Workshops and Classes

Immerse yourself in local culture with our cultural workshops and classes. Learn traditional crafts, cooking techniques, and language basics under the guidance of knowledgeable instructors.

14. Pet-Friendly Accommodations

Bring your furry friends along to enjoy our pet-friendly accommodations. With designated pet areas and services, we ensure a comfortable stay for both you and your four-legged companions.

15. Concierge Services

Enhance your stay with personalized concierge services. From restaurant reservations to local excursion planning, our dedicated team is here to ensure every aspect of your visit is seamless.

16. Eco-Friendly Initiatives

Support our commitment to sustainability with our eco-friendly initiatives. From renewable energy practices to recycling programs, we strive to minimize our environmental footprint.

17. Outdoor Dining Options

Dine al fresco with our outdoor dining options. Whether you prefer poolside snacks, beachfront dinners, or garden views, our diverse dining venues offer scenic settings and delicious cuisine.

18. Art and Photography Exhibitions

Celebrate creativity with our rotating art and photography exhibitions. Featuring works by local and international artists, these displays provide cultural enrichment for our guests.

19. Personalized Guest Experiences

Enjoy personalized guest experiences tailored to your preferences. From special occasion celebrations to custom tours, we go above and beyond to create unforgettable memories.

20. Night Sky Observation

Discover the wonders of the night sky with our astronomy sessions. Join expert astronomers for guided stargazing experiences, where you’ll marvel at celestial phenomena and learn about the universe.
